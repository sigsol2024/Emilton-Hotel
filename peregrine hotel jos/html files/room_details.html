About this suite

Designed for the discerning traveler, the Executive Suite offers a harmonious blend of modern sophistication and timeless elegance. Spanning 45 square meters, this sanctuary features floor-to-ceiling windows that flood the space with natural light and offer breathtaking views of the Rayfield skyline.

Unwind in the spacious living area separate from the bedroom, perfect for intimate gatherings or focused work. The master bath is a spa-inspired retreat, complete with a rainfall shower, deep soaking tub, and premium organic amenities. Every detail, from the 400-thread-count linens to the curated art pieces, has been chosen to provide an unparalleled sense of comfort.
